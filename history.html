Certainly! As a UI/UX designer, there are several essential skills you’ll need to excel in your field. Let’s explore these skills:
1. Prototyping, Wireframing, User Flows, and Mockups:
  • Wireframes: These are web page layouts stripped of visual design, used to prioritize page elements based on user needs.
  • Prototypes: Samples or simulations of final products used for testing and gathering feedback. Low-fidelity prototypes might be sketched on paper, while high-fidelity prototypes allow for mouse and keyboard interaction.
